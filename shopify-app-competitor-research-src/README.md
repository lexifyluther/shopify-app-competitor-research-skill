# Shopify App Competitor Research Skill

Deep competitor marketing intelligence for Shopify apps. Analyze any Shopify app's complete marketing strategy, from paid ads to partnerships, and get actionable insights on what's working and what's not.

## What This Skill Does

Given a Shopify App Store link, this skill produces a comprehensive report covering:

- **14 marketing channels** — paid ads, SEO, content, social, community, partnerships, and more
- **Deep inventory** — specific ads, blog posts, webinars, affiliates, partners, and messaging
- **Metrics & budget estimates** — reach, engagement, spending estimates for each channel
- **Strategy pattern recognition** — identifies if they're using PLG, content-led, paid-acquisition-heavy, partnership-led, or hybrid growth
- **Business stage assessment** — from pre-launch through mature/defend
- **Effectiveness analysis** — which channels are driving results, which messaging resonates, what's underperforming
- **Business impact estimation** — estimated installs, MRR/ARR, growth trajectory
- **SWOT & recommendations** — competitive strengths/weaknesses and actionable next steps

## How to Use

### Quick Start

Paste a Shopify App Store link and ask about their marketing strategy:

```
Analyze the marketing strategy of this app: https://apps.shopify.com/judge-me
```

Or with more specific focus:

```
How is https://apps.shopify.com/reconvert-upsell-cross-sell acquiring new installs? 
What channels seem to be driving growth?
```

### Detailed Prompts

```
Do a full competitor marketing intelligence report on https://apps.shopify.com/example-app
```

```
Compare our marketing approach to https://apps.shopify.com/competitor-app
What are they doing that's working? Where are they weak?
```

```
Research https://apps.shopify.com/example-app paid advertising strategy specifically.
What's their messaging angle? How much are they spending?
```

## What You'll Get

A comprehensive Markdown report including:

1. **Executive Summary** — who they are, their strategy, estimated business stage, key findings
2. **Company Overview** — team, funding, website, other products
3. **Channel-by-Channel Analysis** — detailed findings for all 14 marketing channels
4. **Messaging & Positioning** — key value props, pain points, tone, social proof, CTAs
5. **Strategic Assessment** — growth model pattern, business stage, effectiveness scorecard
6. **Business Impact** — estimated installs, MRR, growth trajectory, market share
7. **SWOT Analysis** — strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, threats
8. **Recommendations** — actionable insights (especially if you share your own app)
9. **Evidence Log** — all URLs, numbers, and specific data points collected

## Tools Used

This skill uses free tools and free tiers of paid platforms:

- **Ad Transparency**: Google Ads, Meta Ad Library, LinkedIn Ads, TikTok Creative Center
- **App Store Intelligence**: SASI, SaaS Insights, App Store Analytics
- **Store/Tech Discovery**: Koala Inspector, StoreLeads, BuiltWith, TrendTrack
- **Affiliate Networks**: Shoffi.app, PartnerStack
- **General Research**: Web search, direct website browsing

No paid subscriptions required.

## Tips

1. **Layer by layer** — The skill works in 3 layers: data collection first, then deep analysis, then strategy. Don't expect instant insight — comprehensive reports take 15-30 minutes.

2. **Be specific** — If you want to focus on one aspect (e.g., "just analyze their paid ads"), say so. The skill will dig deeper there.

3. **Bring context** — If you share your own app name, the skill will provide competitive positioning recommendations.

4. **Some data is hidden** — You can't see their actual ad spend or email subscriber list. The skill makes reasonable estimates based on visible signals.

5. **Evidence over speculation** — The skill prioritizes evidence (specific URLs, screenshots, numbers) over guesses. If something's not visible, it will say so.
