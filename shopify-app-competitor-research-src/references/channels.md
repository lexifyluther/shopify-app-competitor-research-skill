# Channel Checklist — Detailed Research Guide

This reference provides the specific steps, tools, and URLs to check for each of the 14 marketing channels. Work through each channel systematically.

---

## 1. Shopify App Store (ASO)

**What to collect:**
- App title, subtitle, description — are keywords optimized?
- Screenshots and demo video quality — professional or basic?
- Number of reviews, average rating, review velocity (reviews/month)
- How they respond to negative reviews — tone, speed, resolution offered
- Pricing: how many tiers, free plan details, free trial length
- "Built for Shopify" badge — yes/no
- Category placement, any Staff Pick or Featured badges
- Changelog/update frequency — what do updates focus on (features, fixes, performance)?

**Tools:**
- Direct app store listing: `apps.shopify.com/[app-slug]`
- SASI: `sasi.heymantle.com` — search for app, check keyword rankings, category position, review trends
- SaaS Insights: `saasinsights.com` — page views, installs estimate, referrers, keyword tracking
- App Store Analytics: `appstoreanalytics.io` — ASO performance
- Web search: `"[app name]" site:apps.shopify.com`

**Signals of investment:**
- Frequent listing updates, A/B tested screenshots, video professionally produced
- Actively soliciting reviews (in-app prompts, email requests)
- Shopify Referral/Featured = relationship with Shopify team

## 2. Paid Advertising

**What to collect (for each platform):**
- Number of active ads
- Ad formats: image, video, carousel, text
- Ad copy: headline, description, CTA
- Landing page URL for each ad
- Creative themes: what visuals, what messaging angle
- Estimated run duration (how long has each ad been active)
- Targeting signals: who do the ads seem aimed at (job titles, interests)

**Tools:**
- Google Ads Transparency: `adstransparency.google.com` → search advertiser name or domain
- Meta Ad Library: `facebook.com/ads/library` → search by advertiser or keyword
- LinkedIn Ad Library: `linkedin.com/ad-library` → search company name
- TikTok Creative Center: `ads.tiktok.com/business/creativecenter/pc/en` → search keyword or advertiser
- Web search: `"[company name]" ad OR advertisement OR sponsored`

**Budget estimation approach:**
- Count active ads × estimated CPC for the niche ($2-8 for Shopify/ecommerce keywords)
- Long-running ads (3+ months) = likely profitable, higher budget
- Many ad variants = active testing phase, growing budget
- Google Keyword Planner (free) for keyword cost estimates

---

## 3. SEO & Organic Search

**What to collect:**
- Domain authority / domain rating
- Estimated monthly organic traffic
- Top 20 organic keywords they rank for
- Backlink count and top referring domains
- Content strategy: are they targeting bottom-funnel ("best shopify [category] app") or top-funnel ("how to [problem]") keywords?

**Tools (free tiers):**
- Ubersuggest: `neilpatel.com/ubersuggest` — domain overview, keyword rankings, traffic estimate (3 free searches/day)
- Ahrefs free tools: `ahrefs.com/free-seo-tools` — website authority checker, backlink checker
- Google: `site:[domain]` to see indexed pages count
- MozBar (Chrome extension) — free, shows DA/PA
- Web search: `"[domain]" backlinks OR "link to"` for manual backlink discovery

**AI Chatbot Presence (emerging channel):**
- Ask ChatGPT: "What's the best Shopify app for [their category]?"
- Ask Claude: same question
- Ask Perplexity: same question (will include sources)
- Check if the app is mentioned/recommended — this is increasingly how merchants discover apps

## 4. Content & Inbound Marketing

**What to collect:**

### Blog
- URL of blog, number of published posts
- Posting frequency (posts/month)
- Top topics/categories
- List the 10 most recent posts with titles and dates
- Estimated traffic for top posts (use Ubersuggest or web search)
- Content types: how-to guides, case studies, product updates, industry trends?

### Webinars
- Web search: `"[company name]" webinar OR "on-demand" OR "register"`
- Check YouTube for recorded webinars
- Topics, frequency, co-hosted with anyone?

### Podcasts
- Web search: `"[company name]" podcast OR "episode"`
- Check Spotify, Apple Podcasts for company-owned podcast
- Check podcast guest appearance directories
- Topics discussed, audience size signals

### Events
- Web search: `"[company name]" conference OR summit OR "shopify unite" OR event`
- Check if they exhibit at Shopify Editions, Commerce+, ecommerce conferences
- Speaker appearances, booth presence

### YouTube/Video
- YouTube channel URL, subscriber count, video count
- Video types: tutorials, demos, case studies, shorts
- View counts on recent videos, posting frequency

### Newsletter/Email
- Sign up for their newsletter (note the lead magnet used)
- Record welcome email sequence: how many emails, timing, content, CTAs
- Check if they use tools like Beehiiv, Mailchimp, ConvertKit (often visible in email footer)
- Web search: `"[company name]" newsletter OR "sign up" OR "subscribe"`

## 5. Social Media (Organic)

**For each platform, collect:**
- Account URL, follower count, following count
- Posting frequency (posts/week)
- Engagement rate: average likes/comments per post vs follower count
- Content format: text, image, video, carousel, polls
- Top 3 highest-engagement posts (what topic/format worked?)
- Founder personal brand activity (especially LinkedIn)

**Platforms to check:**
- LinkedIn: company page + founder/CEO personal profile
- Facebook: page (not group — that's community)
- Instagram: account
- X (Twitter): account
- TikTok: account (organic, not ads)

## 6. Community & Forums

**What to collect:**
- Community size (member count)
- Activity level (posts/week, replies/week)
- Who manages it (dedicated community manager or founder?)
- What topics are discussed
- Is it used for support, feature requests, general discussion, or marketing?

**Places to check:**
- Facebook Groups: search `"[app name]" OR "[company name]"` in Facebook group search
- Discord: web search `"[company name]" discord OR discord.gg`
- Slack: web search `"[company name]" slack community OR slack.com`
- Reddit: search `"[app name]"` in r/shopify, r/ecommerce, r/shopifyapps
- Telegram: web search `"[company name]" telegram OR t.me`
- Shopify Community Forum: search `"[app name]"` on community.shopify.com — are they actively answering questions?
- Quora: search `"[app name]"` on quora.com

## 7. Affiliate Marketing

**IMPORTANT: Only check platforms where Shopify APP VENDORS list programs (not merchant affiliate tools).**

**Step 1 — Check Shopify-specific affiliate platforms:**
- **Shoffi.app**: `shoffi.app/market` → search for app name. This is the ONLY dedicated Shopify app affiliate marketplace. Check: listed or not, commission rate, number of affiliates, cookie duration.
- **PartnerStack**: `market.partnerstack.com` → search for app or company name. Check Shopify-integrated section.
- **Impact.com**: search `"[company name]" site:impact.com` or check their partner directory.

**Step 2 — Check self-hosted programs:**
- Visit company website → look for `/affiliates`, `/partners`, `/referral-program`, `/affiliate-program` pages
- Check footer links for "Affiliate", "Partner Program", "Earn with us"
- Web search: `"[company name]" affiliate program OR "earn commission" OR "referral program"`
- Check if they use Rewardful, FirstPromoter, or Mantle Affiliate (often visible in affiliate signup page URL)

**Step 3 — Find active affiliates:**
- Web search: `"[app name]" review OR "affiliate link" OR "referral"` — who's writing about this app with tracking links?
- Check YouTube reviews that include discount codes or affiliate links in description

## 8. Partner Ecosystem

**What to collect:**
- Does the app have a formal agency/partner program? (check website for `/agency-program`, `/partner-program`, `/for-agencies`)
- What are the partner tiers and benefits?
- Which specific agencies/consultants publicly recommend this app?
- What technology integrations does the app have? (check app store listing, website integrations page)
- Is there co-marketing with integration partners?

**Where to look:**
- Company website: partner/agency pages
- Shopify Partner Directory (if they're listed as a recommended app by service partners)
- Web search: `"[app name]" agency OR consultant OR "shopify expert" recommend`
- Web search: `"[app name]" integration OR "works with" OR "connects to"`
- LinkedIn: search for people who list the app in their profile/recommendations
- Check if specific Shopify Plus agencies mention the app in their tech stack

**Shopify Partner types to identify:**
- Shopify Service Partners (formerly Experts) — agencies who build stores and recommend apps
- Shopify Plus Partners — certified, enterprise-level, high influence
- Technology Partners — other apps/platforms with bidirectional integrations
- Implementation consultants — freelancers who bundle the app in packages

## 9. Influencer / KOL Marketing

**What to collect:**
- Who has mentioned/reviewed the app (name, platform, follower count)
- Was it paid/sponsored or organic mention?
- What did they say? Positive, negative, neutral?
- Estimated reach of each mention

**Where to look:**
- YouTube: search `"[app name]" review OR tutorial OR demo`
- TikTok: search app name
- LinkedIn: search app name in posts
- Twitter/X: search app name
- Blog posts: web search for reviews
- Check if app has an official ambassador/advocate program

## 10. Directory & Store Intelligence

**This channel has two sub-types:**

### Store Intelligence (merchant discovery: "I like that store → what apps do they use?")
- **StoreLeads.app**: search for app → see which stores use it, install count, install trend (free tier available)
- **Heymantle / Mantle**: `heymantle.com` — app analytics, SASI ranking data
- **Koala Inspector**: Chrome extension — check if app appears prominently when inspecting popular stores
- **Commerce Inspector (Shine)**: similar to Koala
- **TrendTrack.io**: competitive intelligence for Shopify stores
- **Shopgram** (`notify-me.io`): Shopify store intelligence

### Directory & "Top App" Lists
- Web search: `"[app name]" "best shopify app" OR "top shopify app" OR "recommended"`
- Check major list sites: PageFly blog, EcommerceGold, Ecomcy, Avada blog, Shopify blog
- Check "Shopify theme spy" sites — these often include app affiliate links
- Product Hunt: search for app name, check if launched, what score

**Why this matters:**
Merchants often browse successful stores (via StoreLeads, Koala Inspector), see what apps they use, and install the same apps. An app being widely used by "aspirational" stores is a powerful passive acquisition channel. The skill should note how many notable stores use the app and whether this is likely driving discovery.

## 11. Review & Reputation Platforms

**What to collect:**
- G2: `g2.com` → search app name. Rating, review count, badges (Leader, High Performer)
- Capterra: `capterra.com` → search. Rating, review count
- Trustpilot: `trustpilot.com` → search. Rating, review count
- Product Hunt: was app launched? Score, upvotes, comments
- Are they actively managing reviews? (responding, requesting reviews)
- Sentiment trend: are recent reviews more positive or negative than older ones?

---

## 12. Customer Proof & PR

**What to collect:**
- Case studies on website: how many, which customers featured, what results claimed
- Testimonials: where displayed, what metrics quoted
- Media mentions: news articles, industry blog features
- Podcast appearances by founders/team
- Competitor comparison pages: do they have "us vs [competitor]" pages?

**Where to look:**
- Company website: `/case-studies`, `/customers`, `/testimonials`, `/press`
- Web search: `"[company name]" OR "[app name]" featured OR interview OR profile OR case study`
- Web search: `"[app name]" vs OR versus OR compare OR alternative`
- Check if they claim specific metrics: "trusted by X merchants", "generated $Y in revenue"

---

## 13. Referral & Word of Mouth

**What to collect:**
- Does the app have an end-user referral program? (merchant refers another merchant)
- In-app referral mechanics: share link, get credit/discount?
- NPS score if publicly available
- Organic mentions in forums/communities (not company-posted)

**Where to look:**
- In-app: install free trial and check for referral prompts
- Web search: `"[app name]" referral OR "refer a friend" OR "invite"`
- Shopify Community forum: organic mentions by merchants
- Reddit: organic discussions

---

## 14. Acquisition Funnel

**What to collect:**
- Free trial: length, limitations, credit card required?
- Free plan: what's included, what's gated?
- Pricing page design: how many tiers, anchor pricing, enterprise tier?
- Onboarding flow: after install, what's the first-run experience?
- Upsell/cross-sell: in-app prompts to upgrade, related apps promoted?

**Where to look:**
- App store listing: pricing section
- Company website: `/pricing` page
- Install the app on a dev store (if feasible) to experience onboarding
- Web search: `"[app name]" pricing OR "free trial" OR "free plan"`
- Check for comparison calculators, ROI tools on their site
